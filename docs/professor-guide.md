# Professor's Quick-Start Guide: Editing Your Lab Website

Welcome! This guide explains how to update your lab website without needing to know code.

## Logging in

1. Go to `https://hooshmandlab.org/wp-admin/` (or the admin URL you were given)
2. Enter your username and password
3. You're now in the WordPress dashboard

## Adding a News post

News posts appear on the homepage and News archive page automatically.

**Steps:**
1. Click `Posts` in the left menu
2. Click `Add New`
3. Fill in the title (e.g., "Lab Receives NSF Grant")
4. Write the post content in the editor
5. Add a short summary in the "Excerpt" field (appears in homepage preview)
6. (Optional) Add a featured image: click "Set featured image" on the right
7. Click `Publish` when ready

**Tip:** Posts are listed newest first. The three newest posts appear on the homepage automatically.

---

## Adding a Research Project

Research projects appear in the Research section and Research archive.

**Steps:**
1. Click `Research Projects` in the left menu
2. Click `Add New`
3. Fill in the project title (e.g., "Cellular Signaling in Disease")
4. Write a detailed project description
5. Add a short summary in the "Excerpt" field (required for the homepage card)
6. (Optional) Add a featured image
7. Click `Publish` when ready

**Tip:** Research projects display with the newest first. The three newest appear on the homepage automatically.

---

## Editing a page (Team, Publications, Contact, etc.)

**Steps:**
1. Click `Pages` in the left menu
2. Click the page title you want to edit (e.g., "Team", "Publications")
3. Edit the content using the editor (like a word processor)
4. Click `Update` to save

**Formatting tips:**
- Add headings by using the **Paragraph** dropdown and selecting "Heading 2" or "Heading 3"
- Make text bold with `Ctrl+B` (Windows) or `Cmd+B` (Mac)
- Add bullet points by clicking the bullet list button in the toolbar
- Add links by selecting text and clicking the link button

---

## Adding images to a post or page

1. In the editor, place your cursor where you want the image
2. Click the image button (looks like a picture/landscape icon) in the toolbar
3. Click `Upload files` and choose your image
4. (Optional) Add a caption by clicking in the "Caption" field
5. Click `Insert into post`

---

## Managing navigation menu

The top navigation menu shows links to Home, Research, Publications, Team, News, and Contact.

**To change the menu:**
1. Click `Appearance > Menus` in the left menu
2. Select the "Primary Menu" and edit the links or add new ones
3. Click `Save Menu`

(Most users don't need to touch this — it's already set up correctly.)

## Viewing the published site

- Click the site title or logo at the top left of the dashboard to visit the live website
- Or open a new browser tab and go to `https://hooshmandlab.org/`

## Publishing best practices

### Titles
- Use clear, descriptive titles (e.g., "Dr. Hooshmand Presents at AAAS 2024" not "New update")
- Titles appear in email, social media, and search results

### Excerpts
- Write a one-sentence summary that makes people want to click
- This appears as a preview on the homepage and news archive

### Featured images
- Add an image when possible (people respond better to visual content)
- Images should be at least 300×200 pixels
- Keep file size reasonable (under 2 MB)

### Content
- Use short paragraphs (2–3 sentences) for readability
- Add headings to break up long sections
- Include external links to papers, data, or related projects when relevant

## Troubleshooting

### "I published but don't see the update"
- Wait a few seconds and refresh your browser (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Check that the post/project status is "Published" (not "Draft")

### "The page looks broken"
- Click `Update` to re-save the page
- If it persists, contact the developer

### "Where did my post go?"
- Click `Posts` in the left menu
- Use the search box to find it by title
- Check the "Status" filter (it might be in "Draft" instead of "Published")

### "I want to change X but don't see how"
- Look in the right sidebar under "Settings" or "Publish"
- If you still can't find it, feel free to ask for help

---

## What you CAN'T do (and don't need to)

You cannot (and don't need to):
- Change the site design or colors (that's for the developer)
- Upload new plugins or themes
- Edit code (everything is point-and-click)
- Change the domain name or hosting

All of these are developer tasks. If you need changes to any of these, reach out.

---

## Getting help

If something isn't working or you're unsure how to do something:
1. Try the built-in WordPress help (click the "?" at the top right of most screens)
2. Check this guide again
3. Contact the developer
